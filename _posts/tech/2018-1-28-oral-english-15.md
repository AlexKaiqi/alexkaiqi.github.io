## Be into
感兴趣, 喜欢

I'm really into folk music.
我对民谣非常感兴趣.

He is quite into traveling and has been to many different places.
他对旅游十分感兴趣, 已经去过很多地方了.

I kind of have this feeling that he's not that into me.
我有种感觉, 他不是很喜欢我.
